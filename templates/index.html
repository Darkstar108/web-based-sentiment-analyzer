What is Sentiment Analysis?

Sentiment Analysis refers to the usage of natural language processing and text analysis to identify and categorize any given piece of text on how positive or negative the given text is.

How is text analyzed here?

Here text is analyzed using python's TextBlob package. TextBlob gives a polarity score between [-1.0, 1.0] where -1.0 is very negative and 1.0 is very positive. It also gives a subjectivity score of [-0.5, 0.5] where -0.5 is very objective and 0.5 is very subjective.
